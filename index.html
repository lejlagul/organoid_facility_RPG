🧫 Organoid Facility briefing

Can you match a biological question to the right gut-on-chip platform?

You are the Facility recruit. A client brings a biological question. Walk into buildings, collect a gut sample, choose a platform, build the model, analyse it, and make a translational recommendation.

Desktop: WASD / arrow keys + E, Space or Enter. Mobile: tap-to-walk or use the D-pad, then press Talk / Use.
